FAQ


Frequently Asked Questions


  • Do I need to register to view other people's blogs?

    • You do not need to register to be able to view other people's blogs. However registering will make it possible for you to upload your own blogs as well as follow your favourite bloggers.

  • How do I register or create an online account?

    • Log in or create an account using the log in/ create account page.

  • Are my personal details secured here?

    • Yes everything including your phone numbers are secured.

  • Can I delete blogs that I no longer like?

    • Absolutely. You have all the rights to modify your blogs.

  • Can I report or block offensive users?

    • Yes and you are also requested to report on our page so that their accounts are permanently removed.
